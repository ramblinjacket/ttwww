Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Your privacy is simple: Everything you enter stays local on your device unless you explicitly share it. No servers, no analytics, no tracking.

üì± What We Store

Toss Tally stores the following information locally on your device:

Match Information

Team names, player identifiers, scoring records, and gameplay results from your cornhole games.

Player Directory

Optional participant names with calculated statistics to track performance over time.

Application Settings

Visual preferences, team configurations, and match states to personalize your experience.

All information remains in the app's private storage area and is completely removed when you delete the app.

üì° Bluetooth Sharing

Toss Tally offers an optional Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) broadcasting feature that transmits current match details to nearby devices. This feature:

  • Requires explicit user enablement - it's always off by default
  • Operates without internet connectivity
  • Only shares match scores with devices you choose to connect with
  • Can be disabled at any time

üö´ What We Don't Collect

Toss Tally does not collect:

  • Camera footage or photos
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Audio recordings or microphone data
  • Contact lists or personal information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers

We do not integrate any third-party services, advertising frameworks, or analytics libraries. The app uses only native Apple system tools.

üîí Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS security measures including:

  • Device encryption
  • App sandboxing (other apps cannot access Toss Tally data)
  • Biometric authentication (if enabled on your device)
